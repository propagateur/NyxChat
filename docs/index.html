// peer-to-peer · no server · no account

Messages that go straight to the person you trust.

NyxChat is a desktop messenger with no backend to register with or break into. Your device holds your keys, finds peers on your network, and reaches distant friends through Tor. Everything is sealed before it leaves the machine.

What it is

  • A direct line between two devices.
  • Encrypted end to end, on your hardware.
  • Reachable anywhere via a Tor address.
  • Free, open source, yours to inspect.

What it isn't

  • A cloud service with your data on it.
  • Tied to a phone number or email.
  • Routed through a company's servers.
  • Audited yet — treat it as experimental.

// capabilities

Specifics, not slogans.

End-to-end encryption

X25519 key exchange, then every message and file is sealed with XSalsa20-Poly1305 and a fresh nonce. Compare fingerprints to verify a contact.

No server, no account

There is nothing to sign up for and no backend in the path. Your identity is a key pair generated locally on first launch.

Tor onion reachability

Each device publishes a v3 onion service. Friends reach you from anywhere with no open ports, no relay of ours, and no exposed IP.

Automatic local discovery

On the same network, peers appear on their own through libp2p and mDNS. No codes to type, no QR dance for people next to you.

Calls, voice & files

Encrypted WebRTC audio/video with microphone, camera and speaker selection. Voice notes and drag-and-drop file transfer of any size.

Nine languages

English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian. Switched live from settings, detected from your system.

// architecture

Two devices, one sealed channel.

Text and files travel over whichever path reaches the peer; call media stays peer-to-peer. There is no point in the middle that can read it.

macOS says "NyxChat is damaged and can't be opened"?

It isn't damaged — the build isn't notarized with an Apple Developer ID, so macOS quarantines it. Move it to Applications, then run once:

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/NyxChat.app
